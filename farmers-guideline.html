DLK FERTILIZERS FARMER'S GUIDELINE

Getting Started with Our Products

At DLK Fertilizers, we are committed to providing high-quality fertilizers and biostimulants to support farmers in maximizing their yields while maintaining soil health. This guideline introduces our products and how to use them effectively.

From humic acid applications to hydroponic nutrient scheduling, our guidelines are backed by field research and designed for practical, everyday use on the farm.

PRODUCT GUIDE

Understanding Our Products

  1. Soil & Plant Health Boosters
    • Vita Plus (1-1-2): A full-cycle supplement rich in macro and micro-nutrients to promote strong root and stem development.
    • QG Black & QG Gold: Humic and fulvic acid-based chelators that enhance nutrient absorption and plant metabolism.
  2. Growth & Flowering Enhancers
    • Growth Bomb (0.5-4-5): Packed with natural kelp extract and growth hormones that accelerate plant growth and cell expansion.
    • Notorious Bloom (0-50-30): A phosphorus and potassium-rich formula designed to increase flower and fruit size.
    • Hulk It (1-5-4): Encourages large, dense flowers by activating amino acids that promote essential oil and resin production.
    • Bud Life (0-39-25): Provides a phosphorus and potassium boost during early bloom to increase budding sites.
  3. Nutrient Balancers & Supplements
    • Cal Maggie (2-0-0): Supplies optimal calcium, magnesium, and iron to prevent tip burn and improve nutrient absorption.
    • Queen Bee (1-2-3): B-vitamin-enriched formula to enhance root growth and support plants under stress.
    • Carb Bonbon: Essential carbohydrates for energy production and microbial activity.
  4. Detox & Correction Solutions
    • Bud Detox: A specialised flush that removes residual salts and minerals, ensuring clean and high-quality crops.
