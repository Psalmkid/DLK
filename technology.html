The Technology Behind DLK's Products

At DLK Fertilizers, we harness cutting-edge agricultural science to develop fertilizers and biostimulants that enhance plant growth, optimize nutrient absorption, and improve soil health.

Our formulas are designed to maximize efficiency, ensuring that farmers get the most out of every application.

Advanced Nutrient Delivery

DLK's products are crafted using chelation technology, which ensures that essential nutrients—like calcium, magnesium, and iron—are easily absorbed by plant roots. Our QG Black & QG Gold formulas, enriched with humic and fulvic acids, act as natural chelators, enhancing nutrient mobility and uptake.

Biostimulant Power for Growth

Our biostimulants, such as Growth Bomb and Queen Bee, contain marine kelp extracts packed with natural growth hormones (cytokinins, auxins, gibberellins). These compounds promote cell division, root expansion, and stress resilience, leading to faster and healthier plant growth.

Sustainable Soil Enhancement

Healthy soil is the foundation of successful farming. DLK's humic acid-based products, like QG Black & QG Gold, improve soil structure, water retention, and microbial activity, helping farmers cultivate fertile land for long-term sustainability.

Targeted Bloom & Yield Boosting

Products like Notorious Bloom and Bud Life are designed to increase flowering sites and enhance essential oil and terpene production. By using precisely balanced phosphorus and potassium ratios, we ensure that plants allocate energy efficiently for larger, more aromatic, and higher-quality yields.

Smart Detox & Recovery

Over time, plants accumulate excess nutrient salts, which can impact flavor, aroma, and overall crop quality. Bud Detox acts as a plant flush, breaking down residual minerals and improving taste, combustion quality, and post-harvest smoothness.
